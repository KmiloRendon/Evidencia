Monitor

Shows the information of your equipment as images and texts, which are generated thanks to a video card that is inside the computer tower

Mouse

It is a pointing device used to facilitate the management of a graphical environment on a computer.

CPU

The CPU is often colloquially referred to as a microprocessor or simply a processor, and you can think of it as the brain of any computer.

Keyboard

A keyboard is a device that presents the set of keys of various devices, machines and instruments. In general, the keyboard allows the control or command of the device in question.

Electric components

An electronic component is a device that is part of an electronic circuit.

AGP

Is a bus specification that provides a direct connection between the graphics adapter and memory.

Modems

It is a device that converts digital signals into analog (modulation) and vice versa (demodulation), and thus allows communication between computers through the telephone line or cable modem.

Ram Memory

Random Access Memory (RAM) is used as the working memory of computers and other devices for the operating system, programs, and most software.
